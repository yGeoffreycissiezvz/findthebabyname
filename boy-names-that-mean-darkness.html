Boy names that mean darkness

Darkness is the leading boy-side meaning search in 2026. For most parents, it signals night sky, mystery, and strength—not evil.

Three shades of “dark” in baby names

  • Night & stars — Luna’s masculine cousins: names tied to dusk, moon, or black sky.
  • Shadow — Silhouette, quiet power, noir aesthetics.
  • Deep color — Onyx, Cole (coal/black associations in lists), Ebony variants for boys.

Dark first name + bright nickname

Parents worried about playground reactions sometimes use a dramatic full name with a sunny nickname: Onyx “Nick,” Blake “Bee,” Orion “Ori.” Search interest in darkness does not mean you must lean maximalist—meaning can live in the middle name instead.

Culture and spelling

Names from Arabic, Japanese, Gaelic, and other traditions may express “night” or “dark” with nuance English listicles miss. If heritage matters, ask family members for authentic forms rather than anglicized guesses.
